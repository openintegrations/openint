# ✨ Unlock the Power of Magic Links

Use Magic Links to generate unique links for your customers, allowing them to manage their connections effortlessly. These links can also be embedded directly into your app for seamless integration.

## 🔄 How It Works

Discover how to easily access and manage connections without needing a dedicated website. Generate a full connection flow in just a few clicks. No code required.

## 🛠️ Steps to Generate a Magic Link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up or Log In">
    Access your [app dashboard](https://app.openint.dev/dashboard/sign-up) to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure a Connector">
    Set up the desired connector for your integration needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Magic Link Section">
    Click on the Magic Link button/section located on our sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the Link">
    Enter your end-user ID and click on submit. This will generate a Magic Link where you can manage your connections.
  </Step>
</Steps>
