Set the user you want to create the link for and the link duration to generate a full connection flow in just a few clicks. No code required. The best part is that you can embed this into your app!

## üõ†Ô∏è Steps to Generate a Magic Link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up or Log In">
    Access your [app dashboard](https://app.openint.dev/dashboard/sign-up) to
    get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connector">
    Go to Connector Configs in the sidebar and check that you have your desired
    connector setup, if not, please refer to the [Connector
    Configs](/core-concepts/connector-config) guide and come back afterwards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Magic Link Section">
    Go to the Magic Link section in the sidebar. You should see the following
    screen.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/magic-link-form.png" alt="Magic Link Form" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the Link">
    Enter your end-user ID and click on **Submit**. This will generate a Magic
    Link and copy it to your clipboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Adding Connections">
    Open the link in a new tab and start adding connections. Embed the link into
    your app or send it directly to your customers.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/connect-portal.png" alt="Connect Portal" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
