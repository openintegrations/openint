Step-by-step guide to configure a connector in OpenInt.

## What is a Connector?

A connector in OpenInt is a bridge that allows you to integrate with various external services. It simplifies the process of connecting your app to third-party APIs, enabling seamless data exchange and management.

## How to Configure a Connector

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Access the Dashboard" icon="user">
    Log in to your [OpenInt dashboard](https://app.openint.dev/dashboard/sign-up).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Navigate to Connector Configs" icon="globe">
    On the sidebar, click on **Connector Configs** to view available connectors.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Select a Connector" icon="plug">
    Choose the desired connector (e.g., Greenhouse) from the list. Click on it to open the configuration modal.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Configure the Connector" icon="wrench">
    - **Enter Required Details**: Fill in the necessary information such as API keys, client ID, and client secret.
    - **Set Permissions**: Ensure you have the correct permissions set for the integration.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Save and Create a Magic Link" icon="link">
    Once configured, save your settings. You can now generate a Magic Link to manage your connections effortlessly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
