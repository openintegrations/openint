# Connect Acme to Your App

Integrate your Acme account with your app to easily share your profile and manage posts, all within one seamless experience.

## Before You Start

<Note>
To connect, you’ll need an active Acme account.
</Note>

- Make sure you are logged into your app.
- Have your Acme login details ready.

## How to Connect Acme

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the Connection">
    In your app, look for the option to connect your Acme account—usually found in Settings, Integrations, or during onboarding. Click <b>Connect Acme</b>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to Acme">
    A new window will open. Log in using your Acme credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Approve Permissions">
    Acme will ask for your permission to grant your app access to your profile information and posts. Review the requested permissions, then click <b>Allow</b> or <b>Authorize</b>.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish and Confirm">
    Once authorized, the window will close. You will see a confirmation message in your app that Acme is now connected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Permissions

When connecting, your app will request the following permissions from Acme:

- **Read profile information:** View basic information from your Acme profile.
- **Read posts:** Access the posts you’ve made on Acme.

<Info>
Your app only uses these permissions to enhance your experience. Your credentials are never shared.
</Info>

## Testing the Connection

After connecting, try viewing or syncing your Acme profile or posts in your app. If you see your Acme data, the connection was successful.

## Troubleshooting

<Warning>
If something goes wrong, try the following:
</Warning>

- If you accidentally denied permissions, disconnect and try connecting again.
- Make sure you are using the correct Acme account.
- If you're already connected but it isn’t working, try disconnecting and reconnecting.
- Ensure pop-up blockers are disabled or allow pop-ups from your app.
- Still having trouble? Look for a support or help option in your app.

---

If you continue to experience issues, please contact your app’s support team.
